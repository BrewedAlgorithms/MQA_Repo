Standard Operating Procedure: Workspace Setup and Action
Procedure Number: SOP-002

General Requirement:
The first and most critical step is to "open laptop". The laptop must remain open to reference instructions while performing all subsequent actions in this SOP. Therefore, "open laptop" is a mandatory prerequisite for every other step listed below, except closing it at the end.

Step 1: Open laptop.
Step 2: Open Book.
Step 3: Take and use blue spray Bottle.
Step 4: Keep the bottle on the table.
Step 5: Take green bottle.
Step 6: Place bottle on table.
Step 7: Close laptop.
